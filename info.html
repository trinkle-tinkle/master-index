info

"Master index" refers to an index that joins multiple indexes from different Real Books and similar books. The purpose of a master index is to be able to quickly search and find where a tune is located in the different books instead of manually going through the books. It's also useful to find different transcriptions of the same tune: searching for "all the things you are" will return multiple entries of different transcriptions in different books.
